Sports

Tennis

I started playing tennis when I was three years old. From the beginnnig, I really liked the fast pace nature of the sport. When I was around 12 years old, my family and I decided to fully commit to the sport, meaning practising 6 times a week and travelling to tournaments all around the world. Since then I have competed in over 75 countries and played over 200 tournaments. In 2017, I commited to the University of Michigan Men's Tennis Team.
